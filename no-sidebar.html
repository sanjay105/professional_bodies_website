C Programming & Debugging Contest on 23.05.2015

A C Programming & Debugging Contest at department level was conducted on 23.05.2015 at 03:40 to 04:20pm, Under CSI Professional Body Activity for B.E. 1/4 IT(A&B Section) Students. ROUND-I: Written exam-Debugging & multiple choice It was a written test consisting of 20 questions(10 questions are debugging and 10 questions are multiple choice) for 20 marks & the duration is 40 minutes. Written test covers the concepts from the subject: Programming in ‘C’. Total 89 students were participated. Top 6 students were selected for II Round. ROUND-II: Written exam-Programming & debugging It was a written test consisting of 5 questions ( 3 questions are debugging & 2 questions are programming type) for 10 marks & the duration is 20 minutes. Written test covers the concepts from the subject: Programming in ‘C’. Top 3 students were selected for I, II & III Prize winners.

2-Day Workshop on”Web Development with PHP” from 20th -21st Feb, 2015.

Department of Information Technology have organized A 2 Day Workshop on “Web Development with PHP” under CSI student chapter for the students of B.E. 2/4 IT from 20th -21stFeb 2015.The objective behind organizing this workshop is to make the students learn the new scripting language as part of the web technologies subject, so as to build dynamic WebPages. PHP-Hypertext Preprocessor (earlier, it was Personal Home Page ) is an HTML-embedded scripting language used to write dynamically generated pages quickly. PHP has became a powerful tool to build websites and over 300 Million websites are now using it. It is an Open Source and easy to learn.The speaker Mr. sagar Podilapu, web developer, an entrepreneur and the owner of the website www.tutoslive.com handled the session for the two days. A total of 144 students from B.E 2/4 IT A & B participated in the workshop. On the first day of workshop The workshop was successfully handled with the help of the faculty and student coordinators of B.E 2/4 and 3/4 students. The second day workshop began at the same time and the topics covered were MYSQL queries and PHP Database object (Connection between PHP and Database). The speaker carried on the session well for the 2 days. He also agreed to give Merit Certificates to the students who does their assignments within the specified time.

Computer Society of India (CSI)

The seed for the Computer Society of India (CSI) was first sown in the year 1965 with a handful of IT enthusiasts who were a computer user group and felt the need to organize their activities. They also wanted to share their knowledge and exchange ideas on what they felt was a fast emerging sector. Today the CSI takes pride in being the largest and most professionally managed association of and for IT professionals in India. The purposes of the Society are scientific and educational directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science and IT. The organisation has grown to an enviable size of 100,000 strong members consisting of professionals with varied backgrounds including Software developers, Scientists, Academicians, Project Managers, CIO's , CTO's & IT vendors to just name a few. It has spread its branches all over the country. Currently having more than 500 student branches and rooted firmly at 73 different locations, CSI has plans of opening many more chapters & activity centers in smaller towns and cities of the country. The idea is to spread the knowledge, and provide opportunities to as many interested as possible.
